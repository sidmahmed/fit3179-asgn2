Road to migration in Australia

Australia is one of the largest migration destinations in the world. Since 1945, over 7.5 million people have made Australia their home. Parament migrants enter Australia either through the migration program for skilled and family migrants or through humanitarian program for refugees. Migrating to another country is a big step to take. The information here will help you understand your prospects and opportunities.

Start Exploring

Which state offers the highest number of migrations?

When it comes to skilled migration, New South Wales and Victoria have always been the states offering the highest number of migrations. Other states like Queensland and Western Australia used to have low migration numbers but they increased their efforts after 2005 and the numbers have since quadrupled. In 2009 and 2010, there was an overall decrease in the number of migrations due to the global financial crisis. New South Wales, Victoria and Western Australia recovered quickly but Queensland has been more or less stagnant. From 2013 onwards, the states started to become more conservative in the number of migrations they take and they never really caught upto their 2005 numbers.

What type of visa is best for migration?

Skilled visa has always been the most common form of migration. Usually in the cases of families, the primary applicant applies for a skilled visa. Their dependents then apply for a family visa. We can see an overall downward trend in the number of skilled and family visa migrations. Before the COVID-19 pandemic, the Australian Government had already reduced the number of permanent residency visas available from approximately 190,000 to approximately 160,000. In 2018-2019, there were 140,366 visas granted, the lowest figure over the past 15 years. With the Australian borders currently closed, 2020-21 will likely see the lowest number of new permanent visas granted.

Which industries offer the most number of migrations?

Before applying for migration, it is is important to know employers in which industries have historically been more inclined to offer migrations. Accommodation and Food Services employers have historically offered the most sponsorships followed by Professional, Scientific and Technical Services and Health Services. The declining number of migrations in Health Services can be concerning specially during the pandemic. As Australia comes out of COVID-19 lock down and tourists start to arrive again, we could see a spike in the number of sponsorships in Accommodation and Food Services.

Which professions have the best outlook for migration?

Cooks, Software developers and restaurants managers have received the most number of employer sponsored migrations. But if we take a look at independent skilled migrations, we find that Accountants and Registered Nurses have also made the list. This discrepancy could be because the later occupations do not necessarily need sponsorships from employers.

How is the job market for migrants?

There is always a concern within migrants about whether or not they will get a job. And if they do, how long they will be able to keep it. Historically, there has been some discrepancy in the unemployment rate between the Australian born population and migrants with migrants having a slightly higher unemployment rate. But from the graph we can see that this gap is slowly decreasing. Therefore, this should not be a cause for concern.

Can you expect to work in the field you studied?

Another concern many people have is whether or not they might have switch to field different from their background in order to find a job. This is a very legitimate concern and historical data shows that the number of migrants who can find a job in their respective field has seen some decline. But it's not all doom and gloom. Even if they don't find a job in their respective field, there is still opportunities to find jobs in closely related fields.

Which countries are majority of migrants coming from?

Finally, we take look at the countries where majority of migrants are coming from. Historically, most of Australian were from the United Kingdom. But we can see than as time passed, India over took the United Kingdom as the country where most migrants come from. This can be attributed to the technology boom in India and a large number of skilled Indians immigrating to other countries. The number of Chinese immigrants has also increased with China overtaking Philippines in 2012.

Conclusion and References

To sum up everything we have seen so far, Australia is still an attractive migration destination for a lot of people with Victoria and New South Wales being the top two states. Skilled migration is most common form of migration to Australia. Hospitality, professional services and healthcare have historically been the industries of choice for migrants. Cooks, software developers, restaurant managers, accountants and registered nurses are the professions with very good outlook for migration. If you migrate to Australia you can expect to work in the field of your study. And finally, majority of migrants come from India, United Kingdom, Philippines, China and South Africa.
